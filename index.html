Zelda NES Remake

YC Qiu and I reimplemented the first dungeon of Legend of Zelda and included a new, original game mechanic as well.

My Contribution

My contributions include implementing the classic enemies and creating the user interface. I also worked on the health and knockback system. I also worked on several components of the level itself, such as implementing the ability to push blocks. I also reimplemented the Old Man Room.

Pendularia

Pendularia is a thrilling 2D puzzle game in which you guide a ball to an objective with the aid of gravity. Players have to click to hold and swing or quick-pull the ball around the mouse to safely get the ball to the white square. The game's levels will test your skills as you weave the ball to avoid various kinds of obstacles. Are you up to the task?

My Contribution

I created this 2D game from scratch over a 2 week time period. Music in the game does not have copyright and was sourced from freesound.com.
